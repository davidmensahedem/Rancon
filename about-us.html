Who We Are

Rancon Company Limited is a distinguished Ghanaian Building and Civil Engineering Construction Company incorporated as a private limited liability company under the Companies Code 1962, Act 151.

With registration number BN476732013, we have established ourselves as a benchmark for quality, reliability and professional excellence in the Ghanaian construction sector.

I

Our Mission

To deliver quality construction at a reasonable cost with honesty as our hallmark, and to become a Quality Standard Bearer in the Civil Engineering and Construction Industry in Ghana.

II

Our Goal

We aim to be one of the best local construction firms in the country, bringing relief to individuals and corporate bodies who need the services of a reliable contractor by working in an efficient, ethical and balanced manner.

III

How We Work

We plan all aspects of our clients' projects, conducting feasibility studies and impact analysis covering technical, logistical, legal, environmental and financial considerations with experienced professionals committed to quality.
