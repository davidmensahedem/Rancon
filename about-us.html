About Us

Rancon Company Limited is a Ghanaian Building and Civil Engineering Construction Company incorporated as, a private limited liability company under the Companies Code 1962, Act 151 with registration numbers BN476732013.

Our Mission

To deliver quality construction at a reasonable cost with honesty as our hallmark.
and to become a Quality Standard Bearer in the Civil Engineering and Construction Industry in Ghana.

What Our Goal

We aim to be one of the best local construction firms in the country, bringing relieve to individuals and cooperate bodies who need the services of a reliable contractor by working in an efficient, ethical and balanced manner, giving them value for money for their investments embarked-upon, thereby earning the privilege of being the Contractor of Choice.

How We Work

We plan all aspects of our clients' projects, conducting feasibility studies and impact analysis covering technical, logistical, legal, environment and financial considerations. With our well-groomed, young and experienced Construction Professionals, we are fully committed to delivering quality works using great expertise and avant-garge techniques at a minimal cost.
